\chapter*{Notation and reading guide}
\addcontentsline{toc}{chapter}{Notation and reading guide}

The core text assumes high-school mathematics. Optional \emph{Going deeper} boxes
introduce matrix and density-matrix descriptions for readers who want additional
mathematical detail.

\begin{center}
\begin{tabular}{ll}
\toprule
Symbol & Meaning \\
\midrule
$\ket{0},\ket{1}$ & One-qubit computational-basis states \\
$\alpha,\beta$ & Probability amplitudes \\
$|\alpha|^2,|\beta|^2$ & Measurement probabilities \\
$\ket{00},\ket{01},\ket{10},\ket{11}$ & Two-qubit basis states, in this order \\
$H,X,Z$ & Hadamard, bit-flip, and phase-flip gates \\
$\rho$ & Density matrix, used mainly in optional sections \\
\bottomrule
\end{tabular}
\end{center}
